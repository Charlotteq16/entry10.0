Location: Corner of 5th avenue and 14th street, outside the UC

Coordinates:40.73602° N, 73.99339° W

My Thoughts

I have seen these "good luck spots" since my first day at parsons. I continue to see them almost everyday in Union square. While its literally just some words written in chalk on the ground, its the little things like that, that makes me happy. Sometimes I find myself stepping on them on purpose just because it makes me feel good. I know it doesn't actually grant me good luck but a little piece inside of me hopes it does.
